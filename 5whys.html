What is 5 Whys?

The 5 Whys The 5 Whys is a methodical approach to problem-solving that entails asking "why" five times in a row to identify the core of the issue. It's a systematic approach that fosters a greater understanding of the fundamental causes of problems, leading to more workable and durable solutions. By encouraging a careful examination of the problem, this approach avoids a hasty understanding of causation and effect connections.
